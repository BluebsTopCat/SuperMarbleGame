===IMPORTANT THINGS TO KNOW===

You can place down an "Instantlevel" prefab and most things will work out of the gate, but there are a couple annoying things I can't do.

In InstantLevel>Gamemaster>UIPrefab>Eventsystem, you can set the times to get a bronze, silver, and gold medal.

Don't change the "Level" setting until you're done editing things, because whenever you beat the level, it will send info to the high score leaderboard, and it ignores a -1
